Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

Information We Collect

When you request an appointment, subscribe to updates, or contact our team, we collect the personal information you provide such as your name, email address, phone number, pet information, and appointment details. We also log basic usage data (browser type, device, and general location) to keep the site secure and reliable.

How We Use Information

  • Responding to inquiries and scheduling requests
  • Sending appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Sharing important policy or operational updates
  • Improving our services and website experience

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Access is limited to authorized Bow Wow's team members and trusted vendors.

Cookies & Analytics

We use essential cookies to keep the booking experience secure and functional. Basic analytics help us understand which sections of the site are most helpful. You can adjust cookie preferences or block cookies in your browser settings, though some features may stop working.

Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted partners for hosting, email delivery, and appointment communication. These partners are contractually obligated to protect personal information and only use it for the services they provide on our behalf.

Your Choices

You may update or request deletion of your information at any time by emailing hello@bowwowsdogspa.com. We will confirm your identity before fulfilling sensitive requests.
