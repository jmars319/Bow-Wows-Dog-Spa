# Content Reference

This file preserves useful notes from older Bow Wow copy and content-review packages. It is reference material only. Current production copy, contact details, services, prices, hours, social links, legal copy, and launch status must come from client-approved content and the app.

## Useful Positioning Notes

- Position the future full site as warm, caring, polished, and premium without sounding generic.
- Keep the voice friendly, knowledgeable, and lightly playful.
- For the placeholder, keep copy simple: coming-soon status, contact path, and noindex behavior.
- For the full app, booking and service discovery should be clear before boutique/catalog content.

## Visual Direction

- The old reference material suggested a soft premium pet-spa palette with blue, pink, gold, and lavender notes.
- Use real brand assets, client-approved photos, and optimized bundled imagery where available.
- Future gallery/product imagery should remain upload-capable until R2 or another object-storage target is implemented.

## Content Ideas Worth Keeping

- Services should be scannable cards with starting-price language and clear duration/fit notes when approved.
- FAQs should cover vaccinations, scheduling, nervous pets, breed/coat pricing variation, and cancellation expectations.
- Boutique/retail concepts should remain catalog/pre-launch unless checkout is explicitly implemented.
- Future testimonials/reviews should include source context and be kept accurate.

## Retired Or Placeholder Material

- Generated phone numbers, addresses, social handles, service menus, add-on prices, brand claims, and boutique products are not live truth.
- Old recommendations for loyalty/referral systems, dynamic specials, retail checkout, and booking dashboards are future ideas only.
- Any production-facing claim about services, pricing, staff, credentials, or products needs client confirmation before publication.
