Appointment requests are reviewed by our scheduling team. Slots are not guaranteed until you receive a confirmation email. Please give at least 24 hours notice for cancellations so we can offer the time to another pet parent. Repeated no-shows may require a deposit before future bookings.

Pet Requirements

  • Proof of up-to-date rabies vaccination is required for all grooming services.
